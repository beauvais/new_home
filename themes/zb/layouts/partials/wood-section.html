Green woodcraft by Zach Beauvais.

Hand-carved spoons and pole-lathe turned bowls with wooden cups that start from a fresh log.

Spoons

Woden spoons stir your pots, serve your food, and feed mouths.

Bowls

Wooden bowls spin to life on a lathe, and hold your porridge.

Cups

Cups like kuksas are the love-children of wooden bowls and spoons.
